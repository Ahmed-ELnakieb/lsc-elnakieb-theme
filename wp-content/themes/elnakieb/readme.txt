== Description ==
Elnakieb - Solar Energy WordPress Theme

In today's fast-paced digital world, your website is often the first interaction potential clients have with your creative agency. Make that first impression unforgettable with Elnakieb, a collection of premium WordPress themes designed specifically for creative agencies, freelancers, and design studios.

Whether you’re in graphic design, marketing, photography, or any other creative field, Elnakieb offers sleek, innovative themes that help showcase your portfolio with stunning visuals, dynamic layouts, and seamless functionality. Each theme is fully customizable, responsive, and optimized for performance—ensuring your website looks perfect on any device.

Key Features of Elnakieb Themes:
Customizable Layouts: Choose from a wide range of modern layouts that reflect your unique brand identity. Every theme comes with intuitive drag-and-drop builders for easy customization.

Portfolio Showcases: Display your projects in elegant galleries and sliders that highlight your creative work. These themes are perfect for agencies that need to impress clients with an eye-catching portfolio.

SEO & Performance Optimized: Elnakieb themes are built with speed in mind. Lightweight code and SEO-friendly structures ensure your website loads fast and ranks well in search engines.

Advanced Typography & Color Palettes: With access to Google Fonts and a variety of color schemes, you can craft a website that perfectly matches your brand’s style.

One-Click Demo Import: Get started in minutes! Import demo content and templates with a single click, allowing you to set up your website quickly.

Mobile-First Design: Every theme in the Elnakieb collection is fully responsive and mobile-friendly, ensuring that your site looks just as beautiful on smartphones and tablets as it does on desktops.

E-Commerce Ready: Launch an online store effortlessly with WooCommerce integration, enabling you to sell services or products directly from your site.

== Installation ==
1. Upload the `Elnakieb` theme folder to the `/wp-content/themes/` directory.
2. Activate the theme through the 'Themes' menu in WordPress.
3. Navigate to Appearance > Customize to set up your site according to your preferences.
== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= How can I customize the theme? =
You can customize the theme through the WordPress Customizer by going to Appearance > Customize. There, you can change various settings such as colors, menus, logos, and more.
= Does this theme support page builders? =
Yes, Elnakieb is compatible with popular page builders such as Elementor and WPBakery.
